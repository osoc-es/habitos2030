The project. The Change

Covid-19, has had a lot of consequences, mostly negative ones, but some of them positive.
We have seen animals returning to their natural habitats, downfall in contamination due to the less use of cars and less use of plastics due to the lockdown.

Our goal is aimed for a moment of thinking, data analysis and the creation of new measure to maintain those positive habits.
In order to do it, we propose a simple but elegant Web base “App”, in which an every day user goes through a test, and when finished the Web shows some easy-to-do recommendations in order to help the world.

Our Philosophy

The Sustainable Development Goals are the blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all. They address the global challenges we face, including those related to poverty, inequality, climate change, environmental degradation, peace and justice.

The 17 Goals are all interconnected, and in order to leave no one behind, it is important that we them all by 2030 .

Our project tries to achive these main goals
