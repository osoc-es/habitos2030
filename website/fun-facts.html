Fun Facts!


Cheeky cheaters.

Searches on hacks for Moodle and how to copy in Respondus were triggered during the confinement. Although these searches are of a minority volume compared to the searches on Moodle and Respondus, this shows that we Spaniards keep the spirit of the picaresque novel alive centuries later. Legal, but not much.



Let's get cooking

The cheesecake was the cake whose recipe was most often googled during the confinement. All the cakes saw their number of searches skyrocket, but the cheesecake is the champion of our ranking.




Everything's cake now

Cake was the most sought-after dessert in Google's quarantine. Although the peak of popularity was experienced by torrijas during Easter, bizcocho and tarta were more in demand overall.




Runnin', runnin' and runin'.

The treadmill, followed by the exercise bike and the exercise bike were, in that order, the first three purchases during the confinement, according to Google searches in Spain.




Olé!

The YouTube searches of Resistiré and the Dynamic Duo saw their biggest increase in searches since Google recorded data during the month of March, following the start of the lockdown.




A smart hoarder indeed...

An Australian woman put on sale 378 rolls of toilet paper that she bought at the start of the pandemic when she had no place to store them



And speaking of paper!

Did you know that Scottex's profits in the first quarter of 2020 shot up by 45% compared to the same time of year in 2019?


Dark times.

The second week of March was the second worst week in IBEX history). The stock market experienced the worst fall in its history (worse than the crash of 29).

El precio del barril de petróleo en EEUU estuvo en negativo desde las últimas semanas del mes de abril hasta junio, debido a la falta de demanda de petróleo.
